Seminar on Careers in CyberSecurity



A Seminar on Careers in Cybersecurity was arranged on 24th Jan 2022 for the students of the CS&IT Department at NED University.The honorable guest speaker Mr. Mustafa Shiraz Ahmed, Joint Director State Bank of Pakistan shared his insight with the students regarding the career opportunities in this domain.


Session on Data Analytics


An informative session on "Big Data Analytics" was arranged for the students of the CS&IT Department at NED University. The honorable guest speaker, Mr. Shoaib-Ur-Rehman, founder of Virtuosoft, shared his insight with the students regarding the emerging scope of Big Data Analytics.

Session on Emerging Trends on Cybersecurity
